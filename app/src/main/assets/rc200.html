About RC 200

KTM RC 200 is a sports bike available at a starting price of Rs. 2,08,916 in India. It is available in only 1 variant and 2 colours. The KTM RC 200 is powered by 199.5cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 25.4 bhp and a torque of 19.5 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, KTM RC 200 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This RC 200 bike weighs 160.6 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 9.5 liters. KTM has updated the fully-faired RC 200 for 2020 giving it an aggressive-looking paint scheme. It comprises of a black and orange paint along with bold graphics on the fairing. Apart from this, the motorcycle remains the same visually. It gets a dual-LED projector headlamp at the front with LED DRLs that give it a distinctive look. The sleek 9.5-litre fuel tank merges well into the upswept tail section which houses a split-seat setup. The RC 200’s trellis frame continues to house a 199cc, single-cylinder, fuel-injected motor that is now BS6-compliant. However, the power figures remain the same and it continues to churn out 25bhp and 19Nm of torque. It rides on inverted forks up front and a monoshock at the rear. Meanwhile, the braking hardware consists of a 300mm disc up front and a 230mm disc at the rear along with dual-channel ABS as standard. As for competition, the RC 200 goes up against the Yamaha R15 V3 and the Bajaj Pulsar RS200 in the country.
