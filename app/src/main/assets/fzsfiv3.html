About FZS Fi V3

Yamaha FZ S FI is a street bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,04,977 in India. It is available in 6 variants and 7 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,16,833. The Yamaha FZ S FI is powered by 149cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 12.2 bhp and a torque of 13.6 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Yamaha FZ S FI comes up with anti-locking braking system. This FZ S FI bike weighs 137 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13 liters. The FZS FI is one of the most popular model from Yamaha Motor India. And the latest iteration of the 150cc roadster is two kilograms lighter than the 2020 model, tipping the scale at 135kg as against 137kg of its predecessor. The FZS FI competes against the likes of the Honda X-Blade, Bajaj Pulsar NS160, Suzuki Gixxer, and the TVS Apache RTR 160 4V.
